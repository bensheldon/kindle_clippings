Lost in Math

Not only is quantum mechanics itself weird, the research area is too. In particle physics we have theory, experiment, and, in the middle between them, phenomenology. Phenomenologists are the ones who (like Gordy Kane) coax predictions out of theories, usually by simplifying the math and figuring out what can be measured, to which precision, and how (and, not rarely, also by whom).
